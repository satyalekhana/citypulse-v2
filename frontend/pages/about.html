About CityPulse

AI-powered city intelligence for modern travelers

🎯

Our Mission

CityPulse provides real-time city intelligence using cutting-edge AI and MCP Protocol to help travelers make informed decisions.

⚡

Technology

Built with Google ADK, Model Context Protocol (MCP), Groq LLaMA 3.3, OpenWeather API, and Leaflet.js for interactive maps.

🌍

Coverage

Supports 20+ major cities worldwide with live weather, city highlights, cultural information, and AI-powered travel assistance.

🔐

Privacy

We do not store any personal data. All queries are processed in real-time and discarded immediately after response generation.
